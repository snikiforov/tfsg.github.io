HTC Cydget

Новости

Earlier this week we published CyDialer : Call Friends from the Cover Flow LockScreen and Weather Cydget Weather Widget on LockScreen. Here we go with another plugin/widget called HTC Cydget, is a new theme for the lockscreen able to display clock like the HTC phone in real time.
